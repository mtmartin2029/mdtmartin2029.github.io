Road to Intramuros

Origins and Construction

Bus (Most Affordable)
Ride a bus from Dau Terminal to Manila (Lawton/Avenida/Pier)
Fare: ₱170–₱190
Travel time: 2–3 hrs
Transfer to a jeepney/LRT/tricycle to reach Intramuros

Van / Shuttle (Convenient for Groups)
Vans from Clark or Pampanga going to Manila
Fare: ₱200–₱350 depending on operator
Travel time: 2–2.5 hrs

Car / Taxi / Grab (Fastest & Direct)
Direct trip from Clark to Intramuros
Fare: ₱1,400–₱1,800
Travel time: 1.5–2 hrs
