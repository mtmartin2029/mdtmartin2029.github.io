Inside Intramuros: A guide within the walls

INTRAMUROS

Intramuros, meaning “within the walls” in Latin, is the oldest district in Manila and one of the most historically significant areas in the Philippines. Known for its stone fortifications and cobblestone streets, Intramuros served as the heart of political, religious, and cultural life during the Spanish colonial period. Intramuros is more than just a historical site—it is a testament to Manila’s resilience and enduring spirit, connecting the past to the present and offering a unique glimpse into the nation’s heritage.
